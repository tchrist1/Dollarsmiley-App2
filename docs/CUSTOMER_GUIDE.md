## Refunds & Disputes

### Requesting Refunds

**Valid Reasons:**
- Service not performed
- Product not received
- Product damaged/defective
- Product not as described
- Service quality issues

**Refund Process:**

**1. Request Refund:**
```
Navigate to: Order â†’ Request Refund

Select Reason:
- Not as described
- Damaged/defective
- Never received
- Quality issues
- Other

Provide Details:
"Package arrived damaged. Box was crushed and
item broken. Photos attached."

Upload Evidence:
- Photos (required)
- Videos (optional)
- Receipts (if applicable)
```

**2. Provider Review:**
- Provider has 48 hours
- Can accept or dispute
- May offer partial refund
- Or request return

**3. Resolution:**
```
Possible Outcomes:

Full Refund:
- Approved by provider
- Or auto-approved (48 hr)
- Processed in 3-5 days

Partial Refund:
- Negotiated amount
- Accept or dispute
- Fast resolution

Return & Refund:
- Return item first
- Refund after received
- Shipping may be covered
```

### Dispute Process

**If Provider Denies Refund:**

**1. Open Dispute:**
```
Navigate to: Order â†’ Open Dispute

Provide:
- Detailed explanation
- All evidence
- Communication history
- Order expectations
```

**2. Admin Review:**
- Reviewed within 2-3 business days
- Both sides provide info
- Fair resolution sought

**3. Resolution:**
- Admin decision is final
- Refund approved or denied
- Account notes updated

### Refund Timeline

**Credit/Debit Card:** 3-5 business days
**Bank Account:** 5-7 business days
